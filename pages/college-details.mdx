## Add Information
To add information click on the input field and change the information based on the following inputs:

<ResponseField name="College Name" type="select" required>
The school you either attend or graduated from.

Example: `Stanford`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Major" type="text" required>
The degree you are pursuing or have a degree in.

Example: `Engineering`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="GPA" type="text" required>
Your overall college GPA.

Example: `4.0`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Graduation Year" type="select" required>
The year you plan to graduate or the year you graduated.

Example: `2025`

</ResponseField>


<ResponseField name="Athlete" type="select" required>
If you are a college athlete this should always be yes.

Example: `Yes`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Division" type="select" required>
The division of athletics you play / played.

Example: `1`
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="College Award" type="text" required>
A reward you have reieved in college whether it has been athletically or academically.

Example: `First-Team All American`
</ResponseField>
