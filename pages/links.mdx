## Add Linking
To add links click on the input field and change the information based on the following inputs:


### YouTube

To add your YouTube username tap in the field labeled `YouTube`.

### Instagram

To add your YouTube username tap in the field labeled `Instagram`.


## Twitter

To add your YouTube username tap in the field labeled `Twitter`.

### TikTok

To add your YouTube username tap in the field labeled `TikTok`.

### Highlight Links

To add your highlight links tap in the field labeled `Highlight URL`.
