## Add your highschool information
To add your highschool information click on the input field and add the following information:
<ResponseField name="School Name" type="Text" required>
The name of the highschool you graduated from.

Example: `The Canterbury School`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="School Type" type="select" required>
The type of school you graduated from.

Example: `Public` or `Private`

</ResponseField>


<ResponseField name="Graduation" type="select" required>
The year you graduated highschool.

Example: `2021`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="GPA" type="number" required>
The GPA you graduated highschool with.

Example: `3.7`

</ResponseField>
<ResponseField name="SAT" type="number">
Your SAT score if you took it.

Example: `1100`

</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="ACT" type="number">
Your ACT score if you took it.

Example: `22`
</ResponseField>
